Exhibition 1: Digital Dialogues

Digital Dialogues is an immersive exhibition that explores the relationship between humans and technology through interactive installations and digital art. Visitors can engage with AI-generated artworks, participate in virtual reality experiences, and discover how technology is shaping our creative expression.

Exhibition 2: Code & Canvas

Code & Canvas showcases the fusion of programming and traditional art forms. This exhibition features works created using code, including generative art, algorithmic designs, and interactive pieces that respond to visitor input. Explore how artists are using code as a medium to push the boundaries of creativity and redefine the art world.
